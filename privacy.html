PRIVACY POLICY

Your trust is our currency. We are committed to transparency in how we collect, use, and protect your digital footprint within the CashRank ecosystem.

Effective Date: January 26, 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect data in two primary categories:

A. Information You Provide

  • Registration Data: Username, email address, and encrypted password hash.
  • Payment Details: Wallet addresses (PayPal/Crypto) for processing withdrawals. We do not store banking credentials.
  • Campaign Data: URLs and content descriptions submitted for advertising purposes.
  • Support Requests: Information included in tickets submitted to our helpdesk.

B. Automated Data Collection

  • Device Identifiers: Unique Device ID, IP Address, and Advertising ID (GAID).
  • Telemetry: Device model, OS version, battery level, and network type.
  • Activity Logs: Ad impressions, game sessions, tasks completed, and reward history.
  • Security Signals: Data used to detect VPNs, emulators, or rooting tools to prevent fraud.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data powers the ecosystem. We strictly use it for:

  • Service Delivery: Maintaining your coin balance and verifying task completion.
  • Payouts: Processing secure transactions to your requested payment method.
  • Fraud Prevention: Detecting bot networks and ensuring a fair environment for advertisers.
  • Content Verification: Automated and manual screening of submitted URLs to ensure compliance with our Prohibited Content Policy.
  • Personalization: Serving relevant ads based on your general location and interests.

3. Data Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share it in these specific scenarios:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (AWS/Google Cloud) and analytics partners.
  • Ad Partners: Sharing your Advertising ID with networks (AdMob, Unity) to deliver inventory.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by a court order or subpoena to protect our legal rights.

4. Third-Party Integrations

Our App integrates trusted third-party SDKs. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies:

Google Play Services Firebase Analytics Unity Ads

5. Data Security

We employ enterprise-grade encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. However, no digital transmission is 100% secure. You acknowledge this risk when using the Service.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. Transaction logs may be kept for up to 5 years to comply with financial auditing and tax regulations.

7. International Transfers

Your information may be processed on servers located outside your jurisdiction. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of data to the United States and other regions where our infrastructure operates.

8. Children's Privacy

Strict Policy: We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If discovered, such data is immediately purged from our servers.

9. Your Data Rights

You maintain full control over your digital identity within CashRank:

Request Deletion

Go to Settings > "Delete Account" to permanently wipe your profile and balances.

Opt-Out of Ads

Reset your Advertising ID in your Android Settings to stop personalized tracking.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect app changes or legal requirements. Continued use of the App after an update signifies your acceptance of the revised terms.
